About Me

Hi, I'm Alex Morgan, a landscape and portrait photographer based in the Pacific Northwest. For over a decade, I've been capturing the raw beauty of nature and the authentic moments that make life meaningful.

My work has been featured in National Geographic, Outdoor Magazine, and numerous regional publications. I believe in the power of photography to tell stories, preserve memories, and inspire connection with the natural world.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me hiking mountain trails, planning my next adventure, or teaching photography workshops to aspiring photographers.
